Zoey

Character Design

Zoey is a character originally made for Chroma Corps, an art challenge organized by Sinix Design.

Meet The Mighty Zoey! She's a teenage human who was abducted a long time ago and spent her entire childhood trying to find her way back to earth.

That is until she got involved with a little alien called Brutus when they started a partnership in RC car races, winning every championship with good teamwork and a lot of aggressiveness.

The plan was to raise enough money to buy a rocket to send her back to earth, but she ended up enjoying her time up in space.

Invisible

Original Short-Film

Invisible is a 2D frame-by-frame short film released in 2025 as the product of my graduation thesis in Graphic Design School.

It was a daring project, as I challenged myself to be responsible for every animation and illustration step, dealing with a limited timeframe (5 months).

Oi! I´M

Drawing is my passion and my main goal is to evoke the feelings I have for art to those who witness the things I make.

Growing up in Rio de Janeiro, I’ve developed my personal art taste quite soon, as I was fiddling around with graffiti and street art since I was 7 years old, mesmerized by the idea of creating absurd looking characters, 3D letters and blocky shapes. Graffiti was my main source of inspiration until my teenage years as I loved the artistic freedom feeling it gave me, by it’s own transgressing nature.

During the pandemic, art was a coping mechanism during such troubled times, it actually stopped being a hobby as I grew interested in learning it’s core fundamentals, rules and history so I could better my perceptions about what drawing really meant to me - I always wanted to illustrate the reality I see but with a touch of artistic values.

Graffiti also led me into typography studies. I loved creating stylized letters alongside my usual doodles. This got me into studying Graphic Design in college, where I had a few animation classes, which were enough for me to fall in love with the process of making my own animated projects.

In 2025 I graduated Design School with the animated short film “Invisible” as my thesis - unraveling the communication power of illustrated media to deal with sensitive topics. Since then I’ve been working nonstop from animation to varied illustration styles and genres.
